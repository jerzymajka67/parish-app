Our Lady, Queen of Angels

A Catholic Community in La Joya, Texas

Who We Are

Our Lady, Queen of Angels Parish serves the faithful of La Joya and the surrounding Rio Grande Valley. We are a bilingual Catholic community rooted in prayer, celebration of the sacraments, and service to others.

Address: 916 South Leo Avenue, La Joya, TX 78560
Phone: (956) 585-5223

Our Mission

We strive to be a welcoming parish family where all may grow in faith, hope, and love. Through worship, formation, and outreach, we seek to live the Gospel and serve Christ in one another.
